💖 Chhattisgarh emerged as a state from Madhya Pradesh in the year 2000, the state is often unvalued due to the immense problem of Naxalites but here I have scraped together some interesting facts about my state.

💖: Chhattisgarh is the largest producer of Steel in India and produces 15% of the total steel produced in the country. Major mineral resources of Chhattisgarh are Copper, Coal , Iron , LimeStone , Bauxite etc.

💖: Chhattisgarh, being 2nd largest rice producing state in the country is well known as "Dhan ka katora" means bowl of rice. The ancient name of this region is Dakshin-Kausal."Sirpur" a perfect blend of Art , Heritage and History was the capital city of ancient Dakshin-Kausal , it is now located in Mahasamund district from where do I belong.

💖: The state is widely appreciated for a lot of policies for food and health that the state government has passed in favour of it's people. It certainly looks as a State that is concerned in it's people's welfare.

💖: Champaran, the birthplace of Saint Vallabhacharya is located in Raipur, which is the capital city of the state. India's first university dedicated to music and dance , " The Indira Kala Sangeet Vishwavidyalaya " is located in Chhattisgarh.

💖: Anurag Basu, a famous Director and Producer of Bollywood film Industry was born in Bhilai of Chhattisgarh. He has directed films like Life in a Metro , Gangster etc.

💖: Chhattisgarh has high sex ratio of 991/1000 and is ranked as 5th among Indian States. There are 3 National Parks and 11 wildlife sanctuaries in the state.

💖: Some quick facts about Chhattisgarh :

  • Capital : Raipur
  • Lok Sabha Seats : 11
  • Rajya Sabha Seats : 5
  • Created on : 1st November 2000

💖:The state symbols of Chhattisgarh are:

  • State Tree : Sal
  • State Animal : Wild Buffalo
  • State Bird : Pahari Myna

💖: Chhattisgarh shares border with 7 other states , which are U.P , JharKhand , Odisha , Andhra Pradesh , Telangana , M.P and Maharashtra.

💖: It is most densely forested state and is blessed with considerable natural beauty - waterfalls and natural resources . Chhattisgarh is loved by nation for its attributes.
